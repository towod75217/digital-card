🏛About the Firm

Abhishek Yadav & Co., Company Secretaries is a boutique corporate law and secretarial practice headquartered in Lucknow, Uttar Pradesh. Led by CS Abhishek Yadav (ACS, COP No. 28523), the firm delivers end-to-end compliance, governance, and regulatory advisory services to startups, SMEs, and listed entities across India.

With a philosophy rooted in precision, integrity, and client-centricity, the firm serves as a trusted compliance partner — from company incorporation to complex corporate restructuring — ensuring every client stays ahead of regulatory requirements under the Companies Act, 2013 and allied laws.
